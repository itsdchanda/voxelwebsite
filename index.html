Questions? We’ve Got Answers

Here are some common things we get asked. If you’re still curious, don’t hesitate to drop us a message.

What kind of projects do you take on?

We work with startups, brands, creators, and even solo founders. If it’s digital and needs to look, feel, or work better — we’re in.

How do you price your services?

Pricing depends on scope, complexity, and timelines. We offer flexible packages — one-time projects or long-term retainers — tailored to what makes sense for you.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Absolutely. We’re not the type to ghost after go-live. From updates to optimizations, we stick around to make sure everything runs smooth.

How long does a project typically take?

Depends on what we’re building. A basic website might take a couple of weeks; larger platforms or marketing campaigns can span months. We always give realistic timelines.

What if I just need one small thing done?

No problem. Whether it’s a quick edit or a new section, we handle small tasks too — no project is too minor if it matters to you.

Can you handle both design and development?

Yep. We’ve got both brains and hands on deck — from Figma to full-stack, your idea stays in one creative loop.
